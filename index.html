Organization Vision Statement

To empower every person and every organization in North West to achieve more

Organization Mission Statement

To provide thousands of people and businesses with access to Mini Market, Cyber Cafe, Adventure Hub, Tour Guiding, Events Organiser, Equipment for Lease and Maintenance Team in rural and urban communities around North West Province.

Organization Objectives

To buy all the raw materials, machinery, equipment services for the organization

To ensure that the organization has the right quantity and quality of products and services for the organization to achieve its goals

To process the raw materials to ensure that enough production take place to meet the demand of the customer

To carries out market reserve to assess the demand from the customers

To take note of trends in the market and changes in the taste of customers

To keep a close watch over the products and prices of competitors

To determine the price of the products and services and marketing the final products and services to the customers

To keep the customers, suppliers, stakeholders, and anyone involved in the organization happy

To ensure that the is good communication and that these people all know about the success of the organization

To recruit the right people for jobs

To look after the employees or workers in the organization

To make sure that employees are happy and looked after while they are working for the organization

To take responsibility for all conditions of employment

To handle all the money matters of the business

To make the best financial decisions for the organization

To look for possible ways to expand the business and deal with any debts and outstanding payments

To implement, maintaining and controlling technology (systematic application of knowledge) in the organization

To collect, processing and storing all data and information in the organization
